Point of view

Where finance transformation is heading — and what it takes to lead it.

01
GenAI doesn't replace the finance function — it exposes every inefficiency in it Organisations rushing to layer AI onto broken processes will accelerate their problems. The ones that win are those that clean up R2R, P2P and O2C foundations first — then build AI-ready architecture on top. That sequencing is the real transformation mandate for the next five years.
02
The CFO–CIO gap is where most ERP programmes die Technology teams design for system capability. Finance teams protect legacy process. Neither speaks the other's language well enough. The programmes that succeed have someone who can translate — not just manage — between both sides. That is a rare skill, and it is not taught in either discipline.
03
Multi-country transformation is a people problem disguised as a systems problem Across APAC — 15+ countries, divergent regulatory environments, different finance maturity levels — the technology is the easy part. Getting local teams, regional leadership and global governance aligned on a single operating model is where the real work happens.
